My top three movies of all-time

Muhammad the messanger of God

The Messenger of God is a 2015 film that follows the first 13 years of the life of the Prophet Muhammad. The film begins with Muhammad's birth after a great battle for Mecca, and then follows him as he travels with his uncle through the desert in the 6th century. The film also depicts the events of the city of Makkah in the 7th century AD, including the trials and tribulations of the Muslims who are on boycott, and the attempts of Abraha, the King of Habasha, to destroy the Kaaba.

Dune

Power and Suffering. Dune intimately connects forms of power—political, military, religious, economic, and ecological—with human suffering. Throughout the novel, those who seek to hold power are unable to do so without hurting other people.

3 Idiots

The main themes of the movie "Three Idiots" include the importance of authentic education, the negative impact of rote memorization, and the influence of peer relationships on personal and academic growth.
